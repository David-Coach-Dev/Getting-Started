### :zap: Proceso de trabajo de un proyecto de aprendizaje automático

#### Key steps of a machine learning project

- **Amazon / Alexa**

1. Collect data
2. Train model
   - Iterate many timesuntil good enough
3. Deploy model
   - Get data back
   - Maintain / update model

- **Self-driving car**

1. Collect data
   - Image, position od other cars
2. Train model
   - Iterate many times until good enough
3. Deploy Model (Implementar el modelo)
   - Get data back
   - Main / Update models

### :zap: Proceso de trabajo de un proyecto de ciencia de datos

1. Collect data
2. Analyze data
   - Iterate many times to get goof insights
3. Suggest hypotheses/actions
   - Deploy changes
   - Re-analyze new data periodically

## Unsupervised learning (Aprendizaje no supervisado)

Given data (without any specific desired output labels), find something interesting about the data.

Una de las críticas del _aprendizaje supervisado_ es que necesita muchos datos etiquetados.

- Clustering (Segmentacion de mercado)
- Transfer learning
  Learn from task A, and use knowledge to help on task B
- Aprendizaje por refuerzo (Reinforcement learning)
  Use a 'reward signal' to tell the AI when it is doing well or poorly. It automatically learns to maximize its rewards.
  (El aprendizaje por refuerzo es muy eficaz en los videojuegos)
  En el helicoptero autónomo de Standford se usó esta técnica
- GANs (Generative Adversarial Network) - Redes generativas adversarias (Antagónicas)
- Knowledge Graph - Grafos de conocimiento

### Una visión realista de la AI

La IA está teniendo un impacto importante en la sociedad y en la vida de las personas.

**Goldlooks rule for AI**

- Too optimistic: Sentient/super-intelligent AI killer robots comming soon
- Too pessimistic: AI cannot do everithing, so an AI winter is comming
- Just right: AI can't do everithing, but will transform industries

#### Limitations of AI

- Performance limitations
- Explainability is hard (but sometimes doable)
- Biased AI through biased data (Parcialidad)
- Adversarial attack on AI

**Why bias matters?**

- Hiring tool that discriminated against women
- Facial recognition working better for light-skinned than dark-skinned individuals
- Bank loan approvals
- Toxic effect of reinforcing unhealthy stereotypes

**Combating bias**

- Technical solutions
  - E.g 'zero out' the bias in words
  - Use less biased and/or morre inclusive data
- Transparency and/or auditing processes
- Diverse workforce
  - Create less biased applications

#### Ataques adversos a la IA (Adversarial attacks on AI)

Un ataque adversario a un sistema de AI, es un intento que haga algo distinto de lo que se esperaba que haga, con el fin de engañarlo.
